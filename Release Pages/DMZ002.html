DMZ 002

The second DMZ release features the first of many 'Anthems' that would appear on the label. Loefah's contribution, "Horror Show," instigated a turn towards stripped-back, half-step drum patterns within the scene, and the long, dark vocal sample that preceeds the drop would become another staple in the genre. "Lost City" and "Jah Fire" feature heavy basslines accompanied by the eerie accents that would come to define both Mala and Coki tracks. "10 Dread Commandments" is a stomper, with percussion that sounds like it's rattling away in a cave. The dub inspired vocal sample would become a staple on later DMZ productions.

A1: Digital Mystikz - Lost City

A2: Digital Mystikz - Jah Fire

AA1: Loefah - Horror Show

AA2: Digital Mystikz - 10 Dread Commandments
