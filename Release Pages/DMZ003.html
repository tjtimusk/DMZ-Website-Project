DMZ 003

Released in March 2005, DMZ 003 features a number of firsts for the DMZ label. The now iconic 'dmz' logo and label art replaces the white labels of previous releases, while it is also the first release to feature only two cuts, a standard that would be followed in most future DMZ releases. Finally, the tracks themselves are unique in that they are VIPs of previously released tracks.

Mala supplies a VIP of "Da Wrath," which was originally released on another foundational dubstep label, Big Apple Records, in 2003. The 'Souljahz VIP' is distorted and sinister, taking an already heavy track into dangerously dark territory. The same can be said for Loefah's 'Twisup (VIP)', which is actually a remix of the DMZ002 track by Task and Youngsta. Task and Youngsta not only decide to keep the orignal, drum heavy feel of Twisup, but add some flair with bongos and other percussion.

A: Mala - Da Wrath (Souljahz VIP Mix)

AA: Loefah - Twisup (VIP)
